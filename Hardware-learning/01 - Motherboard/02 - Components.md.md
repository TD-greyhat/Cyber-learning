- What is a component?
  The component is a physical part, that making a specific task.

  The motherboard has dozens of components, but there is 10-15 that everybody should know.

- The most important components
	- CPU socket -  CPU goes here
	- RAM slots(DIMM Slots) - Memory moduls goes here
	- Chipset - Coordinating many hardware 
	- PCIe Slots - expansion cards connect
	- M.2 Slot - MVMe SSD connect
	- SATA ports - SATA SSD or HDD connection
	- BIOS/UEFU/Chip - Store the firmware
	- CMOS Battery - BIOS settings, and the timer retention without battery
	- VRM (Voltage Regulator Module) - Handling the energy for the CPU
	- Power connectors - The PSU connection points
	- Fan Headers - fan system
	- Front Panel Header - the engine room buttons (power button LED)
	- USB Headers - obverse USP ports connection
	- Audio Header - obverse audio connection
	- Rear I/O Panel - backpanel connectors (USB, LAN, audio stb..)
	
- What are they doing together?
	for example:. open a picture
	-  The SSD reads in the data
	-  The motherboard forwar the data
	-  The CPU process 
	-  The RAM store it temporary
	-  The GPU outlines it
	-  The monitor show it

	1. Central Processing Unit
